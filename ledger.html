How we check: each forecast gives a likely range. When its date comes, we see whether the real price landed inside that range, and keep a running tally. If our "9 times out of 10" ranges are honest, the price should land inside them about 9 times out of 10 — more often means we're being too cautious, less often means we're too confident.
